About Us

Rajesh Hamal is a Nepalese actor who is considered a superstar in the Nepali film industry. He was born on June 9, 1964, in Palpa, Nepal. Hamal started his career in the entertainment industry in the late 1980s and has since appeared in over 300 Nepali movies. He is known for his versatile acting skills and has won numerous awards for his performances. Besides acting, Hamal is also a goodwill ambassador for various organizations and has been actively involved in philanthropic work. He is widely regarded as one of the most iconic figures in Nepali cinema and continues to be a prominent figure in the industry.
